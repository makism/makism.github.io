Life

I like spending time with a cup of coffee in my hands.

(written after spending some time with a cup of coffee in my hands)

I’m enjoying life with my better half in Maastricht, The Netherlands. I love spending time with her, when traveling (and getting lost with Google Maps), “cooking”, dancing (usually showing off in front of the dj) and watching doggos-related videos on YouTube :D.

Running (something like trailing) is an essential aspect of my daily life. I like early in the morning to have a short run, and during the weekends push for PBs ;)

These days I also like to chill in our small city garden and pretend to be a gardener in Oxygen Not Included.

From time to time, I practice street photography; using a simple Nikon D3300 and a Nikkor 50mm f/1.8G. Although it’s an excellent hobby, it takes lots of time and effort; watch videos, study tutorials, and do the actual shooting.

Another more occasional hobby of mine is gunpla; tried painting but failed, so I stuck with only building and gluing small parts in an irreversibly wrong manner.
